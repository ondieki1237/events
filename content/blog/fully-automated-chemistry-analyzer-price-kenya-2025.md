This guide helps hospitals and labs understand typical price ranges for fully automated chemistry analyzers in Kenya for 2025, factors that affect cost, and what to budget for installation and consumables.

## Typical price ranges

- Entry-level systems: KES 600,000 – 2,500,000
- Mid-range / higher throughput: KES 2,500,000 – 8,000,000
- High-throughput modular systems: Contact for pricing

## What affects the price

- Throughput (tests/hour)
- Number of reagents/channels supported
- Automation modules (sample handling, barcoding)
- Warranty and service package
- Local import costs and shipping

## FAQs

### What is included in the quoted price?

Typically hardware, basic installation, and a starter set of reagents; installation/training and extended warranties may be extra.

### How long does delivery and installation take?

Delivery: 2–8 weeks depending on model and stock. Installation and training: 1–3 days on-site.
