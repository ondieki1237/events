Overview of patient monitor pricing, from basic ward monitors to advanced ICU multi-parameter units, with practical guidance for buyers.

## Price summary

- Basic vital-sign monitors: KES 80,000 – 250,000
- Multi-parameter monitors (ward/OR): KES 250,000 – 800,000
- Advanced ICU monitors: KES 800,000 – 2,500,000+

## Choosing the right monitor

- Consider number of channels (ECG, NIBP, SpO2, Temp, EtCO2).
- Check alarm management and network/HL7 integration if needed.
- Confirm available sensors and spare parts locally.
