This short guide explains typical price brackets for biochemistry analyzers, key features to compare, and tips for procurement teams.

## Price brackets

- Compact analyzers: KES 400,000 – 1,800,000
- Bench-top / medium throughput: KES 1,800,000 – 5,000,000
- High-throughput automated lines: Contact for bespoke quotes

## Buying tips

- Verify local service coverage and spare parts availability.
- Compare throughput against expected test volumes.
- Ask for sample reagent lifetime and consumables costs.
