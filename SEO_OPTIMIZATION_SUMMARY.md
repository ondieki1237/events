# SEO Optimization Summary - Accord Medical Supplies Ltd

## Date: November 11, 2025

## Executive Summary

Based on Google Search Console data showing **27 impressions with 0 clicks** for high-value keywords like "fully automated chemistry analyzer" and poor performance on location-based searches, we implemented strategic SEO optimizations targeting the Kenyan market.

## Key Problems Identified

1. **Zero clicks on high-volume product searches** (chemistry analyzers, patient monitors)
2. **Poor rankings for location-based queries** (Nairobi, Kenya searches at positions 20-90)
3. **Missed opportunities on commercial intent keywords** (equipment suppliers, bulk medical supplies)
4. **Weak internal linking structure**
5. **Insufficient local SEO optimization**
